My Hometown District: Barisal

Barisal is a district in southern Bangladesh, famous for its rivers, lush greenery, and vibrant culture.

Barisal is known as the "Venice of the East" due to its many rivers and canals. It is a major center for rice production and has a rich history in trade and education. The district is also famous for its delicious local cuisine and vibrant festivals.
